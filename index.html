Greenlet — First Private Server

A green-themed remake demo. Register to join and get access to all features.

Register or Login to access your account. The site uses Firebase Auth and Firestore to persist data.

How it works
  • Register → await admin approval (admins set role to member)
  • Buy packs on Market using Tokens (Leafs)
  • Open packs to get Greens saved to your account
  • Chat and trade with other players
